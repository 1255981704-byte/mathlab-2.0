Use the Zeros of the Factors

SETUP & RESULT
THREE FACTORS
\[\frac{2x^2+1}{x(x-1)(x+1)}=\frac A x+\frac B{x-1}+\frac C{x+1}\]
\[x=0\Rightarrow A=-1\]
\[x=1\Rightarrow B=\frac32,\quad x=-1\Rightarrow C=\frac32\]
FINAL ANSWER
\[\boxed{-\ln|x|+\frac32\ln|x-1|+\frac32\ln|x+1|+C}\]
Substituting factor zeros isolates one unknown at a time.

Include Every Power

TEMPLATE & SOLUTION
FOR (X − R)^M
\[\frac{A_1}{x-r}+\frac{A_2}{(x-r)^2}+\cdots+\frac{A_m}{(x-r)^m}\]

Skipping an intermediate power makes the decomposition incomplete.

WORKED EXAMPLE
\[\frac{3x+5}{x(x+1)^2}=\frac5x-\frac5{x+1}-\frac2{(x+1)^2}\]
\[\boxed{5\ln|x|-5\ln|x+1|+\frac2{x+1}+C}\]

Improper Rational Integral

PROBLEM & SOLUTION
INTEGRAL
\[\int\frac{x^3+2x}{x^2-1}dx\]
WORKED SOLUTION
\[\frac{x^3+2x}{x^2-1}=x+\frac{3x}{x^2-1}\]
\[\frac{3x}{x^2-1}=\frac{3/2}{x-1}+\frac{3/2}{x+1}\]
\[\boxed{\frac{x^2}{2}+\frac32\ln|x-1|+\frac32\ln|x+1|+C}\]

VIDEO // IMPROPER RATIONAL

Recombine Before You Trust It

ALGEBRA CHECK
RECOMBINE
\[\frac2{x-1}+\frac3{x+2}=\frac{2(x+2)+3(x-1)}{(x-1)(x+2)}=\frac{5x+1}{(x-1)(x+2)}\]
CALCULUS CHECK
Differentiate the final logarithmic antiderivative.
The derivative must reproduce the original rational function.
This catches sign and coefficient errors.
